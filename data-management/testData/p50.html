Summary

Pros

  • High quality photo results in all test categories
  • Image quality and wide field of view on ultra-wide camera
  • High quality tele zoom
  • Great videos with good stabilization and dynamic range
  • Excellent selfie photos & videos
  • Excellent display experience, particularly in low light and in indoor conditions
  • More than 2 days of autonomy in a moderate use
  • Excellent wired and wireless charging experience

Cons

  • Inaccurate preview : what you see is not as good as what you get
  • No Google Services installed
  • Default brightness is low in outdoor, preventing a pleasant readability of the screen
